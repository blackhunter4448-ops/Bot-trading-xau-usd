Metrics:**\n- **Total Return %**: Overall profit/loss as percentage of initial balance\n- **Initial Balance**: Starting account equity\n- **Final Balance**: Ending account equity\n\n**Trade Statistics:**\n- **Total Trades**: Number of completed trades\n- **Win Rate %**: Percentage of winning trades\n- **Winning Trades**: Count of profitable trades\n- **Losing Trades**: Count of losing trades\n\n**Profit Metrics:**\n- **Average Win**: Average profit per winning trade\n- **Average Loss**: Average loss per losing trade\n- **Largest Win**: Maximum profit in a single trade\n- **Largest Loss**: Maximum loss in a single trade\n- **Profit Factor**: Ratio of total wins to total losses (>1.0 is good)\n\n**Risk Metrics:**\n- **Max Drawdown %**: Largest peak-to-trough decline\n- **Sharpe
